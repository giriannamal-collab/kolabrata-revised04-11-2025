Spreadsheet Chaos

The Problem: Your team uses 15 different Excel files to track orders, inventory, and customer data. When someone updates one file, everyone else has outdated info.

Real Result: A manufacturing company consolidated 23 spreadsheets into one web app. Reduced errors by 85% and saved 15 hours per week.

Field Worker Management

The Problem: Technicians in the field don't know which jobs to do next. Office staff spend hours on the phone coordinating. Jobs get missed.

Real Result: A services company built a mobile app for job dispatch. Workers see their schedule in real-time. Completed 25% more jobs per day.

Customer Self-Service

The Problem: Customers constantly call or email asking for quotes, order status, or to request changes. Your team drowns in repetitive questions.

Real Result: A distributor added a customer portal. Customers now check orders themselves. Reduced support calls by 60%.

Document Generation

The Problem: Creating quotes, invoices, and contracts takes forever. Copy-paste from old documents, fix formatting, update numbers manually.

Real Result: A consulting firm automated proposal generation. What took 3 hours now takes 5 minutes. Sent 40% more proposals.

Route Optimization

The Problem: Delivery drivers follow inefficient routes. Fuel costs are high. Customers don't know when deliveries will arrive.

Real Result: A logistics company added GPS tracking and route planning. Reduced fuel costs by 30% and improved on-time delivery to 95%.

Quality Control

The Problem: Quality checks happen on paper forms. Hard to track trends, find who approved what, or prove compliance during audits.

Real Result: A food manufacturer digitized inspections with tablets. Defects dropped 40%. Audit prep went from days to minutes.
