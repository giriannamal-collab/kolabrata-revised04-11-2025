OUR PROCESS

Our Proven Methodology

Systematic approach that eliminates risk and ensures success every time.

1

Discovery & Assessment

Analyze source systems, data structures, volumes, and dependencies. Identify quality issues and transformation requirements.

2

Strategy & Planning

Design migration architecture, choose tools, plan cutover windows, and establish rollback procedures.

3

Build & Test

Develop transformation pipelines, set up validation rules, and perform multiple trial runs before go-live.

4

Incremental Sync

Perform initial bulk transfer, then continuously sync changes while your team works in the old system.

5

Validation

Automated verification checks every record, relationship, and calculation. Manual spot-checks confirm correctness.

6

Cutover & Support

Execute final sync during maintenance window. Switch systems seamlessly with intensive post-migration support.
